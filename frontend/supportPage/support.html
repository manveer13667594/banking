Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my password?▼

Go to Profile & Settings → Security tab → Change Password. Enter your current password and new password, then click Update Password.

How do I deposit or withdraw money?▼

Visit the Accounts page. Each account card has Deposit and Withdraw buttons. Enter the amount and confirm. The balance updates immediately.

How do I transfer money to another account?▼

Go to Transfer Money, select your source account, enter the receiver's full 12-digit account number in the Beneficiary field, enter the amount and click Review Transfer.

What are the transfer limits?▼

Default daily transfer limit is $10,000. You can adjust this from Profile → Security → Transaction Limits. International transfers may have different regulatory limits.

How do I apply for a loan?▼

Go to the Loans page and click "Apply for Loan". Fill in the principal amount, term, interest rate and purpose. Once submitted, an admin will review and approve your application.

How long does a loan approval take?▼

Loan applications are typically reviewed within 1–3 business days. You can check the status on your Loans page. You will also receive a notification once a decision is made.

How do I pay my loan EMI?▼

On the Loans page, active loans have a "Pay EMI" button. Select the account to deduct from, enter the payment amount, and confirm. The payment will appear in your transaction history.

Is my account and data secure?▼

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We recommend enabling 2FA from the Security settings for extra protection.

How do I download my bank statement?▼

Go to the Transactions page and use the CSV export button to download your statement. You can filter by date range and account before exporting.

What should I do if I notice an unauthorized transaction?▼

Immediately submit a support ticket below with the transaction details. Our team will investigate within 24 hours. You can also call 1-800-BANK-NOW for urgent issues.
